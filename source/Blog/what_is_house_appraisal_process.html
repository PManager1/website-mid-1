what is house appraisal process

Home appraisal is a detailed report of one expert which tells the exact value of your home, Lenders will need third party, objective, certified and neutral appraisers report on the market value of the property before closing. This report will protect and save one from spending more on a home than its actual worth. It contains report on recent sales information for similar properties, current condition,location and opinion of neighborhood. The appaisers just look at the condition of permanent part of the structure or attached to the house. They dont value the decor, furniture or anything fixed to property. Most important part of assessment report is about age of the building , area covered and vacant space like lawns, number of bedrooms, bathrooms ,their size, location, view and their present condition about their repair or paint etc. Measures to boost your home appraisal value : If planning to sell your home, some inexpensive things can be done to enhance your home,s, appraisal value. 1.patch walls and do touch up work to put your home’s best face forward. Fix leaky faucets, broken windows and cracked ceilings. 2. Try to face lift your front doors and get the broken garage’s doors repair along with curbing evergreen landscaping to give a new look. Jot down the repairs and updates you have done over the years and amount of money spent on it. Try to display the importance of your neighborhood pride such as new roads, schools, market etc to impress the appraiser. Thus home appraisal process is a final report on the value of the property. It will provide total analysis and valuation report to the lender and to the owner of the home. The final detailed report will cover valuationof the following details. Location, size and condition of the house. Comments on structure and its problems like cracked foundation s or wet basements area. Renovation details done recently. Status condition of the permanent fixers, such as lights, ceiling fans, air conditioners etc. Comments on the surrounding area and its locational advantages. Photographs, maps and sketches of property in question. Detailed market analysis of the area and house in question for { seller. }
