How do I sell my house without an agent ?

It is a very difficult option to sell your house without an agent because it is combersome process to finalize the deal. But a dedicated and motivated home owner can save commission to be paid to the agent by following these steps to sell his home of his own way as under: Its advisable to hire a real estate attorney who will stood with the owner in case of need. Only legal fee is to be paid instead of agents commission. However one can consult one or more real estate agent to discuss value of his property, chances of sales or on other issues to get an experience ,One can get higher value or lower by deducting agents fee of 6% or more. But its not always clear that your home will be sold at higher price through the agent it can be otherwise. Valuation of your home: To arrive at true value of your home one can hire a good real estate appraiser. He will give the true pictureof value of your home by comparing with recent sales in your area through many websites data and through his personal experience of real estate business. Clear the mess from your home: Its very important to give a clean look to your home by clearing it from extra furniture, broken/ useless goods lying in the house. Normally the US spring season is considered as the best time for sale or just before the schools begin their classes of school going children. weather related websites or newspapers can guide you to list your home for sale. In addition to above one should prepare a list of updating and improvements of home done. There should be a detailed information of local area about schools, roads, transportation and market etc and very clear about disclosures which can be prepared through / assistance of a legal or real estate professionals. Thus for selling a home you are required to give a good exposure of home that can be through advertisement in news papers, signboards, thorough websites like zillow, hotbeds, Trulia etc. If the buyer is serious he can write up offer and deposit in a letter of contract with assistance of a real estate attorney, The contract should be accompanied by a mortgage pre-approved from a qualified lender with initial deposit of cheque. The contract and closing paper work should be checked by attorney.Be sure about mortgage papers and deed prepared rightly to safe guard your interests. ensure that your buyer agent arrange for title insurance. Also get a certificate of occupancy from the buyer. Get appraisal report of buyerâ€™s bank with the help of your own appraiser to finalize the deal. After finalization of deal the home owner should remain vigilent till he gets final payment from the buyer. One should keep the house intact as the buyer has seen it before all negotiation and vacate the house before the day of occupation by the buyer.
