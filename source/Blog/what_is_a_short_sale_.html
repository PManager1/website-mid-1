what is a short sale ?

Short Sale A short sale is a transaction in which bank lets the delinquent homeowner sell the home for less than what’s owed. The borrower finds an agent and put the house on market, often at a substantial discount. If the house sells the lender ( banks ) will recoup the majority of what the homeowner owes. It saves the lender from expenses of a foreclosure suit and possible longterm cost of owning a hard to sell foreclosure home. The short sale does not make free the borrower from the debt he or she incurred with original mortgage, but it can be better than a full on foreclosure. The short sale normally present a better option than letting your home slide into long, draining process of foreclosure. The investor prefers short sale proposals than foreclosure deals to work fast. But to succeed in short sale you require skill and techniques to satisfy and influence both buyers/ seller and lenders in the process. 1)Presentation skills of property in question. 2)Complete knowledge of property assessment. 3)How to meet the needs of homeowner and the lender. 4)Technique to move faster in the short window time. Equipped with the understanding one can save a lot of time and avoid any critical error before taking class on the subject for first offer. Working smarter is equivalent to working faster for reaching to the final decision on the matter. The first step in the short sale investment process is to identify the property headed for “auction”. It should be property that homeowner does not prefer to lose but can’t retain and the bank would prefer not to own. The ideal homeowner can’t find way out of foreclosure and bank is open to short sale to get rid off money pits of the foreclosure process but if you manage to bargain at a lower price then you can be a perfect “short sale” candidate. How to locate such Houses : Direct route to identify foreclosure bound properties is officiel notices published in legal section of newspapers, visit county court houses for list of pre-closure properties. Try to contact credit cousellers, real estate agents and attorney to tell them that you are looking for short sale opportunities. A successful short sale mean,you are getting an amazing deal and paying less on a price of property that is of more worth and everybody is happy over negotiations / participation in the deal. Do a thorough search before making any offer on any pre-forclousre property. There may be a debt attached to the property that you will have to pay including liens and fines, late fees, older mortgages and even late utilities. Be sure you did thorogh inspection of exterior and interior to know what repair expenses you have to pay before making the house a home habitable. Also find out if any home improvements are subject to city approval .thus a number of these unexpected expenses could completely wipe out any profit you expect from a short sale, so be sure to do that home work . The importance of agents representing the homeowner can’t be ignored. These are real estate agents who specialize in fore closure propeties. They can be well versed in the art of short sale proposals and can become your biggest allies in future trasactions also. Thus to be an expert in short sale in real estate matters one has to be expert, intelligent and conversant with the latest reports of sales relating to pre-forclosure properties with additional closeness and understandign with the real estate agents. Thus experience of negotiation and awareness of property business is a masterstroke for successful deals in short sale property business. 2nd. ..The process and benefits of dealing with cash home buyers. ... People sometimes have an idea to sell their house quickly and list their house for cash home purchase for certain reasons. 1) They might be interested to shift to another city or location bcz of better job opportunities,good weather,better law and order or better educational facilities for their children or plan to live near their close friends.2)certain people may sell their house quickly to avoid complications of fore closure .3) may plan to sell their house to cash home buyers after their retirement to finish their liabilities or plan to shift to small house to reduce their expenses or feels to shift to their near and dear ones in old age . 4)even after divorce some immediately plan to sell their house to cash home buyers.separation will force them to distribute their wealth between them very soon to avoid future litigation. 5)Even sudden death of a person may force their heirs to sell property soon to avoid troublesome problems of mortgage etc. 6)Sometimes troublesome neighbors may force to sell house to cash home buyers to live peacefully.Thus process of selling a house to quick cash home buyers is normally easier. All formalities of listing,engaging real estate agents are not required to be followed which takes at least 3 months to a year to settle quick sale of their property.But if location of house is good the deal will mature very shortly. Generally cash home buyers pay less than the market value but property can be sold soon to meet demands of seller.It saves time as it takes only one to two weeks to settle the sale process. Selling house through cash home buyers is more secure than banks or financial institutions. Cash home buyers takes responsibility of renovation, repair, cleaning etc which disburdens the owner. Thus choosing to sell yr house through cash home buyers will save levy of about 6% generally charged as commission by real estate agents. Moreover engaging money home buyers will save expenses on brokerage and adds etc.
