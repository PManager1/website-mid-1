How to prepare your house for sale ?

Most stressful events of life are divorce and selling a home or other property is too painful during the period of divorce settlement .Home is a valuable, important and greatest asset that the couple has which carries emotional and sentimental values of life. One may like to retain or dispose off due to financial ,legal or personal problems. This process haunts personal life for many years. The couple can opt the following options during the process of divorce. One of the spouses can retain the house by settlement of other legal interest through mediation wiht their attorney. Selling a house during the process of litigation is most tricky process thus an agreement before divorce decision is best if possible to avoid further frustration. Sometimes home can be sold when youngest child reach eighteen. Selling the marital home will allow you rebate upto { $500, 000 } in profit from federal capital gain taxes, couple can apply for tax break if they file joint or separate returns provided they qualify for two out of five years in home qualifications. Thus selling a house while getting divorce needs to be planned carefully HOW TO GET HIGHER SALE VALUE ? Selling an ideal and well located house in the midst of divorce can be very unfortunte, but clarity on divorce is must before proceeding further . One may get lower price on divorce issue but way get better price if oneâ€™s reply to the buyers questions are clear and convincing . Try to have following information on your tips. : Copies of tax returns, property tax payments/ fee for the last year. List of items in the house such as electric gadget, fans, furniture etc. Copies of loans accounts, its balance, interest rate, monthly payment detail and terms of assumptions etc. Copies of bylaws of housing society or associationo to which your house belongs Must have knowledge about defect of the house if any. Detail of utility bills. List of utility items installed and their energy saving benefits. List of security system installed in the house. Professional appraisal, title of insurance policy, equipments, warranties detail, map of the area where house is located, schools ,shopping center, transportation facilities, parks, health center etc. Infact presentation of property for sale is important to attract the buyers and get a good value.
